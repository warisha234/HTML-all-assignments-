BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS:
  • Chicken 1/2 kg
  • Onions 3 or 4
  • Tomatoes 4
  • Potatoes (optional) 2
  • Oil 1/2 cup
  • Ginger & Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
  • Red Chili Powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander 1 bunch
  • Fresh Mint 1/2 bunch
  • Yogurt 1/2 cup

Method:
  1. Instruction:

    Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
    Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Cook the rice:

    In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked and drained rice with whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon), and salt.
    Cook until 70-80% done. Drain the rice and set aside.

  3. Fry the onions:

    In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil. Fry thinly sliced onions until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

  4. Layering the biryani:

    In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread a layer of marinated chicken at the bottom. Top with a layer of cooked rice.
    Add fried onions, coriander, mint, and green chilies. Repeat the layers and finish with rice on top.

  5. Cooking the biryani:

    Cover the pot tightly with a lid or almunium foil. Cook on low heat for about 20-
    25minutes , or until the chickens is cooked through and the flavours have melded together You can also place a tawa (griddle) underneath the pot to prevent burning.
